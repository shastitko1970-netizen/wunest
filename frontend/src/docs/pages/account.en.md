# Account & billing

The `/account` page surfaces everything about your WuNest + WuApi profile: wu-gold balance, tier, model activity, gold-transaction history.

## Personal info

The header shows your name or username from WuApi (fetched via `/api/me` on every load). Below it — your registration date. These aren't editable here — profile management (name, avatar, OAuth links) lives in the WuApi cabinet.

The **Refresh** button reloads all blocks (profile, stats, gold) from the backend — handy after a top-up or a manual edit in the WuApi cabinet.

The **Sign out** button clears the `wu_session` cookie (across all `*.wusphere.ru` subdomains) and best-effort POSTs to WuApi for server-side session invalidation. Then it does a hard-reload to `/` so the SPA's auth-check runs fresh and shows the sign-in screen.

## KPI grid (4 cards)

### Gold balance
**wu-gold** is WuNest/WuApi's internal currency. 1 gold = $1 USD at the provider's rate. Stored with 9-decimal precision (nano-units), displayed with 8 decimals (`1.23456789`).

Every WuApi-pool request to a model debits the real upstream USD cost (OpenRouter / Anthropic / Google etc.) → converted to gold with half-away-from-zero rounding. **The minimum paid request is always ≥ 1 nano** — tiny costs never round silently to zero.

The **Top up →** button takes you to the WuApi cabinet (`wusphere.ru/dashboard`) where top-ups happen via YooMoney / Telegram Stars / Crypto.

### Tier
Free / Plus / Prem / Pro Plus / Max. Free is the minimum — a daily request limit + a restricted model list. Paid tiers drop the daily cap and unlock premium models (Claude Sonnet 4.5, GPT-4o, Gemini Pro, Wu-named tunes).

Underneath the tier name — its expiry (`until 2026-05-25`) or `no expiration` (lifetime / admin-grant). The **Manage →** button leads to the WuApi cabinet's subscriptions page.

### Today
How many requests you made today + how many remain from your daily limit. Counts WuApi-pool chats only — BYOK chats don't count against the daily limit; their billing is entirely provider-side.

### Referrals
How many people came in via your link. Referral payouts are a WuApi feature — this card is just a counter.

## Token usage
Day / week / month / total counts of `tokens_in + tokens_out` since account creation.

## Top models
Top 6 models by your request count. For each — `today / this week / total` counters. Useful to see what you actually use — often one model takes 80% of requests and BYOK becomes worth it for that one.

## Gold history
Table of the last 50 transactions. Columns:
- **Date** — debit/credit timestamp
- **Description** — kind: `purchase` / `spend` / `refund` / `admin_grant` / `promo` / `dedupe_replay`
- **Model** — for spend transactions, the model that used it
- **Tokens** — in / out
- **Δ** — balance delta (+/−) in gold
- **Balance after** — state after this transaction

History is live up to **24h TTL** on our end — older rows expire from our server, but **the wu-gold balance never goes anywhere**, the full ledger lives in WuApi.

## WuNest access (beta)

WuNest is in closed beta — only users with a valid `nest_access_granted` flag can use chat. Without the flag, you'll see an access-code form on Account:

- One-time codes (24 hex chars), generated by the admin in batches
- On redeem: flag flips on your WuApi user + the code is stamped used
- After redeem, the field collapses into a small "Access activated" badge

If you didn't come in through a referral and didn't get a code — ping [@wuapi_support](https://t.me/wuapi_support).

## Theme recovery (if the UI broke)

If an imported ST theme or your custom CSS broke the interface enough that you can't click into Settings — Account has emergency buttons:

- **Clear custom CSS** — wipes your Appearance's `customCss` (background and colors stay)
- **Safe mode** — reloads with `?safe=1`. Skips ALL: preset CSS, customCss, bg image, persisted Vuetify theme. Returns to bundled default (nestDark) until you drop the param

More on safe mode → [`safe-mode`](safe-mode).

## Delete my account

Account deletion lives on the WuApi side (`wusphere.ru/dashboard` → Settings → Delete Account). Deleting your WuApi user cascades to all WuNest data (chats, characters, BYOK keys, presets, lorebooks).

If you want to reset just your WuNest data but keep the WuApi account — that mode doesn't exist yet, ping support.
